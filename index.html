ODD SIGNAL█

TECHNOLOGY CONSULTING FOR ARTISTS & INDEPENDENTS

Odd Signal works with artists, small studios, and independent practitioners who need technology to actually work for them — quietly, reliably, without a team of engineers to maintain it.

We handle the technical side so you don't have to. From hardware wired into sculpture to privately-hosted AI running your admin — the goal is always the same: technology that disappears into your practice.

Automating the boring parts. Bringing AI into a commission without losing the art. Rebuilding the website that embarrasses you. Or anything else — if it has a power button, it's in scope.
